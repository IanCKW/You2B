Features

  1. To add videos, see Connect Playlist for instructions
  2. To remove videos from your feed, simply click the "-" button below it
  3. All removed videos will be committed to your account if you change page (month-year buttons at the top), reload the website or logout.

Connect Playlist

Adding a playlist will allow you to continually add videos to your you2b account whenever you are browsing the main YouTube website. Simply create a playlist in your main YouTube account, navigate to your account library -> playlists -> the playlist you would like to connect. Once at the playlist, copy the everything in the url after list= and paste it into the bar above.

Now, whenever you are scrolling through YouTube and you find a video you would like to watch later, you can add it to your connected playlist and that video will show up in your You2B account with the rest of your videos!

These added videos will be pushed to the front of your feed the first time they are loaded into your account, and they will be assigned the date that they are first loaded in. Thus, they will not clog the front of your custom feed over time.
