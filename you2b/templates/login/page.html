Welcome to You2B
Guide
Features
  1. To add videos, go to the main YouTube website and add them to the "Watch Later" playlist
  2. To remove videos from your feed, simply click the "-" button below it
  3. All removed videos will be committed to your account if you change page (month-year buttons at the top), reload the website or logout.
  4. GitHub repo: https://github.com/IanCKW/You2B
Getting In
  1. If you arrive at the "Your connection is not private" page, click on the "Advanced" button the bottom left
  2. A small link will pop up below. Click to proceed
  3. Grant all permissions that are requested to use You2B
